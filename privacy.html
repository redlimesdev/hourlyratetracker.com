Privacy Policy

TCKR  ·  Effective date: June 2026

No data collected Works fully offline Nothing shared
TCKR does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device only.

1Introduction

Thank you for using TCKR. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2Who We Are

TCKR (Hourly Rate Ticker) is an independent Android application that shows your live earnings as a single, growing number. It is built for freelancers, consultants, and anyone who works at a fixed hourly rate. The app is available on the Google Play Store.

3What Data We Collect

TCKR collects no personal data whatsoever. Specifically, the app does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any other identifying information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Financial information
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

All data you enter in the app — such as your hourly rate, currency, and chime settings — is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

4Internet Access

TCKR does not connect to the internet. The app functions entirely offline. No data is sent to or received from any server, cloud service, or third party.

5Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your Android device using standard Android storage. This data includes:

  • Your configured hourly rate and currency preference
  • The current timer state and elapsed time
  • Your chime preferences (interval and chosen sound)
  • Your appearance and widget settings

This data remains on your device until you reset it within the app or uninstall the application.

6Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers involved in this application.

7Custom Chime Sounds

TCKR lets you choose your own sound for the optional earnings chime. If you select a sound file from your device, it is accessed solely to play your chosen chime and remains under your full control. We do not upload, copy, or have any access to your sound files.

8Home Screen Widget

The optional home screen widget displays your timer status and earnings directly on your device. All widget data is generated and stored locally; nothing is transmitted anywhere.

9Children's Privacy

TCKR is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. Since the app collects no data at all, it poses no privacy risk to any age group.

10App Permissions

TCKR requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function. Any permission related to storage or audio is used solely to let you select and play your own chime sound on your own device.

11Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be made available on the Google Play Store listing. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Google Play Store listing for TCKR.
